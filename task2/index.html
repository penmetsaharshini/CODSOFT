Care Guides

01. Lighting

Provide bright, indirect sunlight. East-facing windows are perfect for most indoor species.

02. Watering

Check the soil. If the top 2 inches are dry, it’s time to hydrate your plant friend.

03. Humidity

Tropical plants love mists. Use a humidifier or mist the leaves daily during dry summers.

04. Nutrition

Feed with organic liquid fertilizer once a month during the growing season (Spring/Summer).
